The journal 11.11 re-emerges into the world after a one-year hiatus.
The year of thinking about reading and writing, about ecologies and
reading ecologies, brings us to this day, 11.11.18 when the magazine
once again shows its face to The World. The intention of our editorial
team is to offer readers a chance to read, online, “cover to cover” and
to have that great feeling of having read “cover to cover.” Over the
next months, as we push towards our second issue, designed around
ideas of Ecopoesis, we will continue to explore what it means to read
on the screen. As I write this, people continue to talk about whether
our faces are too enmeshed with screens. The goal of this magazine
is to make each second of that joining of brain to code a deliberate
act. And a beautiful one.
Talk to me.
Leslie Carol Roberts, Editorial Director
